The Story of Ambattur Singam Logistics

How one man's hustle on a TVS XL bike grew into a multi-million dollar logistics and tech company.

Logistics Tech Mobile App

The Spark of an Idea

Ambattur Singam was a man with style, charisma, and an eye for opportunity. While sitting at a local tea stall, he noticed a universal problem: small businesses struggled with reliable, timely deliveries. He saw a gap in the market and decided to fill it himself. With his trusty TVS XL bike and a catchy tagline, "Faster than your mom calling you for dinner," Singam Speed Logistics was born.

"I didn't have a business plan. I had a bike and the belief that I could do it better than anyone else. That was enough."

Hitting the Streets

In the beginning, Singam was a one-man army. He handled every pickup and delivery himself, building relationships with each customer. His infectious energy and commitment to service quickly made him a local legend. Businesses that were once hesitant to trust a newcomer were soon relying on him for all their delivery needs. His personal touch was his greatest asset.

Scaling with Technology

As demand grew, Singam knew he needed to innovate. He invested his earnings into developing the Singam Express App, a platform that allowed customers to book and track deliveries with ease. The app was a game-changer, catapulting his business from a local service to a regional powerhouse. With over 5 million downloads, the app solidified his company's position as a leader in last-mile logistics.

Today, Ambattur Singam is a celebrated entrepreneur, proving that with enough grit and a smart embrace of technology, anyone can turn a simple idea into a thriving empire.
